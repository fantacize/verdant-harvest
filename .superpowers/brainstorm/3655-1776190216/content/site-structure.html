Solstice Farm — Site Architecture

Full site with 6 pages. Modern & clean aesthetic. Built for a 15-min presentation.

Pages

1

Home

Hero with tagline, mission snapshot, seasonal highlights, featured products, CTA to shop/subscribe

2

About / Our Philosophy

Farm story, organic principles, team/founder section, sustainability commitment. Key assignment content: why organic, why local, why less meat

3

Products

Seasonal produce grid (spring/summer/fall/winter), weekly veggie box subscription, pricing tiers

4

Sustainability

Carbon footprint data, local vs imported comparison, meat industry impact stats, plant-based diet benefits. Heavy assignment content lives here

5

Events

Upcoming events calendar — farm tours, cooking workshops, sustainability talks, seasonal festivals

6

Contact

Location map, hours, contact form, newsletter signup, social links

Navigation
